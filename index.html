About me

I’m an Honours Bachelor of Commerce student (option in Business Technology Management) at the Telfer School of Management, University of Ottawa, graduating in 2028. I’m especially interested in how AI, cybersecurity, and digital identity intersect with policy, ethics, and real-world user needs.

Outside the classroom, I actively participate in case competitions, hackathons, and innovation challenges that push me to build solutions for sectors like healthcare, tourism, and cybersecurity. I enjoy synthesizing research, structuring messy information, and telling clear stories through data, design, and presentations.

I’m also deeply involved in student support and mentorship: helping international and business students navigate academics, careers, and life in Canada through coaching, peer support, and community-building.
